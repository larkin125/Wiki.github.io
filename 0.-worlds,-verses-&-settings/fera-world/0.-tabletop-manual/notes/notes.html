Vitality

Represents:

  • Stamina
  • Energy
  • Minor bruises
  • Near misses

Vitality returns relatively quickly. A veteran boxer might lose most of his Vitality but remain largely unharmed.

Wound Severity Effect
Minor -1 physical actions
Moderate -2 physical actions
Severe Movement reduced
Critical Risk of death

Trauma

Permanent or semi-permanent damage.

Examples:

  • Missing eye
  • Damaged lung
  • Crushed hand
  • Severe burns

Trauma requires major treatment and may never fully heal.

Blood Loss

Blood Loss affects:

  • Consciousness
  • Stamina
  • Recovery
  • Death risk

Healing

This is where Fera could become very unique.

Natural Healing

Safest method.

Benefits:

  • No side effects.

Downsides:

  • Slow.
  • Requires rest.
  • Requires food.
  • Requires clean conditions.

Medical Treatment

Doctors, surgeons, haematurges.

Benefits:

  • Faster recovery.
  • Reduces complications.

Risks:

  • Infection.
  • Failed procedures.
  • Blood loss.

Alchemical Healing

Benefits:

  • Fast.

Risks:

  • Addiction.
  • Organ damage.
  • Dependency.
  • Toxicity.

Magical Healing

Fastest.

But not free.

  • Extreme pain.
  • Fatigue.
  • Tissue damage.
  • Accelerated ageing.

So a soldier might survive a battle thanks to magic, but pay a price later.
