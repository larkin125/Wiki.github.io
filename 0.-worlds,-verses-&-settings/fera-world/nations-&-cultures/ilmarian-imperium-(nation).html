Summary

The Ilmarian Imperium is the Eastern Nation of The Three Kingdoms. It is ruled by an Imperator and is a Constitutional Monarchy with its people commonly known as 'Marians'.

The Land is split into various Principalities with the capital city of the Imperium being Ilmaria (City). The Imperium is split into various Principalities, Colonies and Protectorates

The Nation is ruled by The Imperator but has a Parliament split between two different Parliament called The Parliament of Princes and The Parliament of the People with the The Parliament of Princes made up of the Prince's of the various Principalities and the The Parliament of the People made up of voted figures from Non-Lorded titles.

The Military of the Imperium changed at the start of the 1800's into the Ilmarian Expeditionary Force, prior to that it was simply known as The Ilmarian Imperial Army.
