Ashta

Summary

Found during a storm outside The Monastery of the Atiporith by one of the Monks, Ashta grew up not knowing her race or her home. After 16 years of living at the monastery, she began to show an nack for the darker and more emotional sides to the Aether and was banished after an attack in which she slaughtered the bandits raiding their home.

Not far from the place she had lived for all her life, the monk was caught by Slavers and sold into Slavery. Soon bought as a new slave for The Ring-Dang-Do at the price of Fifty-Five thousand Crowns, the most famous house of Entertainment and pleasure in The Principality of Adestein's capital, the girl soon rose from Back girl to Front girl and would soon run the establishment with her husband. Although she would have to earn back her fee with the deal given to all slaves bought by The Ring-Dang-Do; “Earn your price thousand fold and you are free.”

Ashta would need to earn herself 55 million Crown to earn her freedom.

Appearance

Ashta is a Ikini, a species characterized by their head-tails. She has orange skin with white facial markings, and her head-tails are striped with blue and white. Ashta's eyes are blue, and she has a strong and determined expression.

Personality

Ashta is known for her independence and strong-willed nature. Even as a young trainee, she often displayed a determination to prove herself and make her own choices. Ashta is a highly skilled combatant with her swords.

Ashta cares deeply for others, and her compassion is evident in her interactions with friends, allies, and even adversaries. Ashta is loyal to her friends and allies, forming strong bonds, her loyalty is a defining trait, and she is willing to take risks to protect those she cares about.
