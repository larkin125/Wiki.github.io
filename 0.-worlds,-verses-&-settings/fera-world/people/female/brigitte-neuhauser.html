Summary

Protagonist of the Story Project Kriegerin (Novella)

Appearance

Brigitte has long burnt orange hair and dark blue eyes. Brigitte's hair is loose, with the flanks held in twin tails with her red Interface Headset. She wears a school uniform as her most iconic outfit, which consisted of a dark blue pleated overall dress over a white button-up blouse with short puffed sleeves, a red bow attached to the collar, white knee socks with red stripes and black shoes. She wears a red watch on her wrist.

She is the pilot of a Kriegerin Automata called Valkyrie.
