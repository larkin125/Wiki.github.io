Hildegunde von Vernichtung

Summary

Colonel Hildegunde is a decorated soldier of the Ilmarian Imperium (Nation), known for her exceptional tactical acumen and ruthless efficiency on the battlefield. Despite her youthful appearance, being only 14 years old, she is one of the Imperium’s most formidable military assets, embodying a disciplined, pragmatic, and often ruthless approach to warfare.

Hildegunde was inducted into the Ilmarian military at an early age, quickly rising through the ranks due to her prodigious combat abilities and strategic brilliance. Her talent in both magical and conventional warfare has made her a legend among her peers, though many outsiders underestimate her due to her childlike appearance.

As one of the Imperium’s finest soldiers, Hildegunde is frequently deployed in key military operations, serving as both a frontline officer and a symbol of Ilmarian might. While her expertise lies in battle, she is sometimes assigned to diplomatic missions as a show of strength—despite her personal disdain for politics.

Personality

Hildegunde is calculating, disciplined, and unwaveringly loyal to the Imperium. She harbours a deep cynicism toward diplomacy, believing in the superiority of military force as the ultimate tool of statecraft. Her demeanour is cold and professional, but those who cross her soon learn that her appearance belies the lethal efficiency of a hardened soldier.
