Summary

William Blake is a writer down on his luck. With a string of successful books, he has struck upon the foul curse of Writers Block. His wife, Rebecca Blake, struggles to help her husband deal with it and it is straining their marriage. Then walks in an old flame.

Emma Jackson was Blake's University fling and mysteriously vanished one day without a trace, now she's back just as the rocks start to appear in Blake's marriage.

Unfortunately for Blake, he ends up trapped in Limbo, a world powered by creativity layered beneath our own and must write something to help himself escape. Constantly returning to 'The Writers Room' a place of seemingly solidity in the void of Limbo, Blake makes this his own Citadel of Darkness.

Part 17: The Cult of the Word

Part 1: The Ritualistic Murder

Detective Richard 'Dick' Head is called to the scene of a gruesome murder in an abandoned church. The victim, a young woman, is found with strange symbols carved into her body. As Dick investigates, he discovers that the symbols are linked to an ancient cult known as "The Cult of The Word. With a series of mysterious, ritualistic murders baffle the city.

Part 2: Dark Secrets Unveiled

Dick delves into the victim's background and uncovers a web of connections to a secret society that operates under the guise of a literary club. As he delves deeper, he realises that members of the cult are prominent figures in the community, making his investigation increasingly dangerous.

Part 3: Cryptic Writings

During his investigation, Dick stumbles upon a series of cryptic writings that seem to hold the key to the cult's activities. The writings are filled with ominous prophecies and rituals that hint at a dark and ancient power. Discovering a connection between the murders and the writings of an author named Blake. The eerie alignment of the crimes with Blake's stories unnerves him. He also uncovers unsettling connections between the cult and Blake's stories, with cryptic messages hinting at a deeper, more sinister plot.

Part 4: The Enigmatic Leader

Head stumbles upon a clandestine gathering where the cult enacts their gruesome rituals inspired by Blake's writings.
Dick identifies the enigmatic leader of the cult, a charismatic figure known as "The Scribe." The Scribe is a master manipulator, using his charm to draw in unsuspecting members. Dick becomes obsessed with uncovering The Scribe's true identity and motives.
A cat-and-mouse game ensues as he narrowly escapes, leaving him haunted by the eerie faces hidden behind hooded robes.

Part 5: The Chosen One

As Dick gets closer to the truth, he discovers that the cult believes in the arrival of a "Chosen One" who will bring about a new era of darkness. The recent murder is revealed to be part of a series of rituals aimed at summoning this malevolent force.

Part 6: The Haunting Rituals

Dick infiltrates one of the cult's secret meetings, witnessing their disturbing rituals and ceremonies. The cult members speak in a strange, ancient language that echoes through the hidden chambers of the old church, sending shivers down Dick's spine.

Part 7: A Trail of Betrayal

The investigation takes a personal turn when Dick's closest ally on the police force is revealed to be a member of the cult. Now, Dick must navigate a treacherous path, unsure of who he can trust within the department.

Part 8: Unleashing the Darkness

The cult's activities escalate, and Dick realises that time is running out. The Chosen One is on the verge of being summoned, and the city is in grave danger. As he races against the clock, Dick faces supernatural occurrences and hallucinations, questioning his own sanity.

Part 9: The Confrontation

Dick confronts The Scribe in a final, climactic showdown within the depths of the abandoned church. The cult leader reveals the true nature of the Chosen One and the apocalyptic consequences that will follow its arrival. It becomes a battle of wills as Dick fights to prevent the cult's dark prophecy from coming true.

Part 10: The Aftermath

In the aftermath of the confrontation, Dick must come to terms with the horrors he has witnessed. The city is forever changed, and the cult's influence lingers. As Dick grapples with the psychological toll of the case, he wonders if the darkness that the cult unleashed can ever truly be vanquished.
